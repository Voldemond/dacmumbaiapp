Our Courses

Programs Offered

  • PG Diploma in Advanced Computing (PG-DAC): Designed to provide in-depth knowledge of software technologies.
  • PG Diploma in Embedded Systems (PG-DESD): Focuses on embedded system development and applications.
  • PG Diploma in Big Data Analytics (PG-DBDA): Equips students with skills for big data processing and analytics.
  • PG Diploma in Artificial Intelligence (PG-DAI): Covers machine learning, deep learning, and AI algorithms.

Eligibility Criteria

Graduates in Engineering/Technology, MCA, or other equivalent disciplines are eligible to apply.

Admissions

Admissions are based on a performance-based entrance test conducted by CDAC.
