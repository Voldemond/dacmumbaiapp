Our Mission

The Center for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) Juhu is dedicated to empowering students with cutting-edge technical knowledge. We aim to nurture future leaders in the fields of software development, artificial intelligence, big data analytics, and embedded systems.

Why Choose CDAC Juhu?

  • State-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty
  • Industry-aligned curriculum
  • Strong placement support with top IT companies
  • Focus on hands-on learning and project-based approaches
